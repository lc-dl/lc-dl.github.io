What is LC-DL?

LC-DL is an app downloader made specifically for LiveContainer, built entirely in iOS Shortcuts. LC-DL supports downloading tweaks or app data and can even add apps to your homescreen automatically with LC → HS

How to setup LC-DL.

1. Tap on the "Tap to download LC-DL" button above.
2. Setup the shortcut by selecting the LiveContainer folder on your iDevice.
3. Download apps!
